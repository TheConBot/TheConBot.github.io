Drop_Block!

Drop Block is a minimalist block stacking game where you stack as many blocks as you can in order to reach the objective. The game features three different game modes with over 30 levels and an endless stacking mode where you can compete with your friends for the high score! Drop_Block! was made in one month by only myself and is deployed on the Google Play and App stores.

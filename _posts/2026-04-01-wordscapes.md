### Description
Wordscapes is a AAA, award winning, and incredibly popular word game franchise available on [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.peoplefun.wordcross) and [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/wordscapes-word-game/id1207472156)!

### Contributions
I was incredibly fortunate to work directly with the team on the Wordscapes port from the jMonkeyEngine to Unity 2022, with my role being to own implementing bedrock level platform sdks to accelerate the game team through their launch. This included business critical features such as identity, analytics, remote configs, segmentation, experiments, and ads. The project was a huge success, with us accomplishing the ultimate goal of eliminating years of mounting tech debt and setting the team up for success in growing the brand. 

I also worked on other games in the Wordscapes/Peoplefun portfolio such as Wordscapes: Solitaire, Wordscapes: Search, Blockscapes, and many more!

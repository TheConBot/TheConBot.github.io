### Description
PeopleFun's Platform Tech product is a collection of client packages and backend services covering core infrastructure for a AAA mobile studio's entire portfolio. Major features include networking, identity, analytics, ads, storage, consent, remote config, experiments, segmentation, cross-promo, iap verification, and more.

I was proud to work with this team for over three years, moving from a contract based position to full time employment.
